project 1

NOR Gate (Mini project)
The NOR gate, a fundamental component of digital logic circuits, operates on the principle of Boolean algebra to produce an output signal only when none of its inputs are active. It functions as an inversion of the OR gate, generating a low output only if all input signals are high. This gate finds extensive use in digital electronics for its versatility in constructing logic circuits and is particularly essential in the design of memory units, arithmetic circuits, and microprocessors. Its simplicity and effectiveness make it a cornerstone in the development of various electronic systems, contributing significantly to the advancement of modern technology.
